Climate Receipt

Rising temperatures, rising concerns; our planet's warning is getting louder.   Rising temperatures, rising concerns; our planet's warning is getting louder.  

Based on real-time temp data

***A graph illustrating the rise in average temperatures, comparing data from 2005 to 2023.***

The font is inspired by alarming data from the Alessandro Serpieri Meteorological Observatory and real-time temperature data from the OpenWeatherMap’s API. The heaviest font weight corresponds to the highest recorded temperature in the city [39.4°C in 2022], while the lightest weight reflects the lowest recorded temperature [-12.4°C in 1985].
